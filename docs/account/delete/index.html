Delete Your Account

Effective date: 11 May 2026

You can permanently delete your Quantum Cube account at any time. Deletion is immediate, irreversible, and removes all personal data we hold about you.

How to delete your account from the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly from inside the Quantum Cube app:

  • Open the Quantum Cube app and sign in if you are not already signed in
  • Tap the gear (settings) icon in the lower-left corner
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all associated data are removed immediately on confirmation.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the following from our systems:

  • Your profile record (name, date of birth, email address)
  • Your authentication record (sign-in credentials)
  • Your purchase and unlock status
  • Your marketing consent preference
  • Any readings history or personal data associated with your account

Reading content itself is generated from your inputs at runtime and is not stored, so nothing else remains after deletion.

This action is permanent

Deletion is permanent and irreversible. We cannot restore a deleted account or recover any data associated with it. If you have an active purchase, you will lose access to your lifetime unlock and will need to purchase the app again if you want to use it in future.

If you cannot access the app

If you are unable to sign in or otherwise cannot reach the in-app deletion option, email us and we will process your request manually:

Email: support@quantumcube.app
Subject: Account Deletion Request

Please send your request from the email address associated with your Quantum Cube account so we can verify your identity. We will confirm completion by reply within 7 business days.

Data retention after deletion

After deletion, the only records we may retain are minimal transactional records required by law (for example, payment receipts retained by our Merchant of Record, Dodo Payments, for tax and accounting purposes). These records do not include passwords or active account access.

More information

For a full description of how we collect, use, and protect your data, see our Privacy Policy.
