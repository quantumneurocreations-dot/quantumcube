Delete Your Quantum Cube Account

You can delete your Quantum Cube account at any time. This page explains how, and what happens to your data when you do.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Open the Quantum Cube app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (top-right of the screen).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.
Note: Account deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your email address and account profile
  • Your unlock/payment status
  • Your marketing preferences

We do not store birth dates or names after a reading is complete — these are processed in-session only and never persisted.

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at admin@qncacademy.com with the subject line "Delete Account" and the email address associated with your account. We will action your request within 7 days.
